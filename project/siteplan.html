Urban Brew

Site Plan

Site Name

Urban Brew

I chose "Urban Brew" because it balances the modern, city-centric lifestyle with the artisanal craft of coffee making. It implies a place that is convenient for professionals yet high-quality enough for enthusiasts.

Site Purpose

The website will be a single-page application designed to showcase the coffee shop's brand and products. It will include a 'Hero' section with a call-to-action, an informational section about the shop's amenities (like Wi-Fi and beans quality), a visual menu gallery displaying items and prices using CSS Grid, and a footer containing the location, hours of operation, and social media links.

Scenarios

Target Audience Question 1:

"Do you have options for vegans and can I work there with my laptop?"

Answer: The 'Amenities' section will explicitly list free high-speed Wi-Fi and our selection of alternative milks (Oat, Almond, Soy) to reassure remote workers and vegans.

Target Audience Question 2:

"Where are you located and are you open on Sunday mornings?"

Answer: The Footer will be prominent on every view, containing the address (with a map link) and a clear list of operating hours for every day of the week.
